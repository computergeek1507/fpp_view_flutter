Privacy Policy

Controller Viewer · Effective June 11, 2026

In short: Controller Viewer does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. It has no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything it stores stays on your device, and it only talks to the Falcon Player (FPP) controllers on your own local network.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Controller Viewer application (the “App”) handles information. The App lets you discover and control Falcon Player (FPP) devices on your local network.

Information we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data with the developer or any third party. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics or tracking SDKs, no crash reporting, and no advertising.

Information stored on your device

The App saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, so it can remember your setup between launches:

  • The list of FPP controllers you add or discover (such as their hostnames or IP addresses) and related display preferences.

This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us or anyone else. You can erase it at any time by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App.

Network access

The App communicates only with devices on your local network in order to function:

  • Discovery — it uses local multicast/mDNS to find FPP controllers on the same network.
  • Control & status — it sends HTTP requests directly to the FPP controllers you choose, to read their status and send commands.

These requests go straight to your controllers on your local network. The App does not route your data through any server operated by the developer.

Permissions

On Android, the App requests only the permissions needed for local-network communication:

  • INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to send requests to controllers and detect connectivity.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE and CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE — to discover controllers via multicast on Wi-Fi.

The App does not request access to location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or files.

Children’s privacy

The App is a utility for managing lighting controllers and is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and posting the revised policy at this URL.
